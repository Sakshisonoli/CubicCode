About Cubiccode Digital Media LLP - ISO 9001:2015 Certified Company.

The Story of Cubiccode

Cubiccode Digital Media LLP is the brainchild of Sarvesh Kakkeri and Shivani Jadhav, cofounders who embarked on this incredible journey in 2016. Starting from a small office, we envisioned creating a digital transformation for businesses through innovative web solutions. Our expertise lies in three core services.
Websites and Web Application Designing and Development, E-commerce and CMS Website Development, Search Engine Optimization (SEO).
In those early days, we were driven by a simple yet powerful vision which we still follow: to build websites that are not just attractive but also responsive, fast, reliable, and efficient. Our ultimate goal was to help businesses boost their sales and digital presence.

Our Journey From Visionaries to Digital Innovators

From our humble beginnings, we quickly made a mark in the industry. By 2018, we had served over 150+ clients, delivering exceptional web solutions. In 2019, our hard work and dedication were recognized when we were named one of the 10 Best Web Development Start-ups in India by Silicon India. Additionally, we were honored with the title of Most Outstanding Web Development Company in Karnataka at the AI Global Excellence Awards and rated as the Best Website Designing and Development Company in Belgaum, Karnataka.
Our growth continued as we expanded our services and client base. In 2020, our cofounder Mr. Sarvesh Kakkeri delivered an inspiring TEDx Talk, sharing our journey and vision with a broader audience. Our achievements have been featured in top news publications such as Hindustan Times, Business Standard, YourStory, ANI, and more.

Our dedicated team of developers, designers, and project managers work tirelessly to exceed client expectations. We pride ourselves on delivering websites that not only meet but surpass industry standards.

We provide solutions for your creative ideas.

At Cubiccode Digital Media LLP, we believe in continuous innovation and excellence. Our vision is to empower businesses by providing cutting-edge web solutions that drive growth and success. We are committed to being at the forefront of digital transformation, helping our clients achieve their goals through superior web development services.
